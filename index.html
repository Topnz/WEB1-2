Welcome to Riftforge — the crucible of creation within the League of Legends universe.

Here, imagination takes form and new legends are born. Whether you're a lore enthusiast, a designer at heart, or a strategist dreaming of the next meta-breaking idea, Riftforge gives you the tools to breathe life into your own champions.

Craft their abilities, define their roles, shape their stories, and build the world around them. From forgotten gods of the Freljord to cunning inventors of Piltover, your champions can come from anywhere in Runeterra — or from realms yet unseen.

Join a growing community of creators, share feedback, explore others' designs, and refine your concepts together. The Forge is open to all who dare to create. Step forward, Summoner… your legend begins here.
